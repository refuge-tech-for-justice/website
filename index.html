About


CentRefuge provides a technical solution to the current refugee crisis. Unlike many other platforms, CentRefuge allows refugees to communicate their basic needs and also invites volunteers to respond to those in need. Our project is threefold: a SMS system, an Android application, and a web applcation.

The SMS system allows refugees to request food, water, or shelter through texts. By texting +1-(256)-515-4199, they can easily connect with volunteers in the area who can provide them with the help they need. Our system is scalable and fault-tolerant - we built a distributed system based on master and slave servers to ensure that no requests are ignored and our volunteers are always kept up-to-date.

The Android application retrieves the requests from our databases and notifies nearby users if there's someone that they could help near them.

Finally, the web application populates and visualizes the requests from the refugees. By aggregating this data, we can better understand the areas which are truly in trouble - where the volunteers simple cannot keep up to the need. By pinpointing these areas, we hope to make it easier for authorities to respond promptly and effectively.
